# Transient IV Measurement for FET (1ms/poin)

## Requirements:

### 1. CHA設定Gate PULSE(orDCmode)電壓5V,pulse on 5ms/ off 2ms 兩發後都給0V,

### 2. CHB 設定Drain DC電壓5V,持續量測ID,1ms(或更快)一個點,持續量一分鐘.
